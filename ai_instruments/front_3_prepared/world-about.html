About This World

The Lost City of Eldoria is a mystical ancient city floating among the clouds, where magic and technology intertwine in perfect harmony. This extraordinary realm exists suspended between earth and sky, held aloft by ancient arcane energies and sophisticated gravitational manipulation systems.

Here, scholars of the arcane work alongside brilliant engineers, creating wonders that blend mystical forces with cutting-edge innovation. The city's inhabitants have mastered the delicate balance between preserving ancient magical traditions and embracing technological advancement, resulting in a unique civilization unlike any other.

History & Origins

The Great Ascension (Era 0)

Originally built on solid ground, Eldoria was lifted into the sky during a cataclysmic event known as the Great Ascension. The city's most powerful mages and engineers combined their knowledge to save their home from a devastating earthquake that would have destroyed it entirely.

The Harmony Wars (Era 150-200)

A period of conflict between traditionalist mages and progressive engineers nearly tore the city apart. The resolution led to the founding of the Synergy Council, which established the principles of magical-technological cooperation that define Eldoria today.

The Golden Integration (Era 300-Present)

The current era of unprecedented cooperation between magical and technological disciplines. Major innovations include the Cloud Bridges, the Floating Markets, and the revolutionary Aether-Tech hybrid systems that power the city.
