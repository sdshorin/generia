About Lyra

Lyra Starweaver is one of Eldoria's most innovative Arcane Engineers, specializing in the fusion of magical energies with mechanical systems. Born into a family of traditional enchanters, she broke convention by studying both mystical arts and engineering at the Academy of Convergent Sciences.

Her groundbreaking research focuses on crystal resonance technology, which allows magical energies to power complex mechanical devices. She's particularly known for her work on the Cloud Bridge stabilization systems and the development of the first magically-enhanced construction automatons.
